Why use Linkey?

Protect sensitive information with enterprise-grade security in just a few clicks.

Zero-Knowledge

We don't store your data. Encryption happens locally in your browser before the data ever touches our servers.

RSA Cryptosystem

Utilizes asymmetric encryption. Only the holder of the private key can decrypt the message you send.

Ease of Use

No account required. Just drag, drop, and generate a secure one-time link for your recipient.

How it Works

Three simple steps to secure your data.

1

Write Secret

Type your sensitive data or password into the secure text area.

2

Add Public Key

Upload the recipient's RSA public key to encrypt the data specifically for them.

3

Share Link

Generate the link. The data can only be viewed by the private key holder.
